Arsanesia Game Studio



I was an intern in Arsanesia as a game programmer in 2017. I was involved to code some function and gameplay for 2 games, "Pippo Belajar Huruf" and "PTown/Pippo Town" (Now it's called "Summer Town"). Here are some game functions I made for those games:



Pippo Belajar Huruf

This game is about learning the alphabet, teaching kids about letters in a fun way. For this game, I made 3 gameplay: Write the letters, Arrange the letters, and Match the letters.

  • Write the letters is a gameplay where the player learns to write letters. The function tracks player's touch positions and draw the line following them. To write letters, the player have to draw the lines in the letters area.
  • Arrange the letters is a gameplay where the player learns to place letters in the right order to form a word. The player plays by drag a letter in the screen and drops it in the right place of the word.
  • Match the letters is a gameplay where the player learns about uppercase and lowercase letters. The player plays by drawing the line from an uppercase letter to a lowercase letter. The function will check if it's the correct match between the two letters.



PTOWN

PTown is a game about virtual pet. In the game, players can create their own pet, raise and take care of it. Players can buy clothing, food, toys and house furniture for their pet. For this game, some functions I made are: Create Pet, Furniture Behaviour, and Pathfinding.

  • Create Pet is a function for players to create their own pet. Players can select facial parts of the pet (eyes, nose, mouth, etc.) and change it to the one they want. Players can also change the color of their pet.
  • Furniture behavior is a function to handler how furniture works. Furniture used by players to decorate their pet house. There are 4 types of furniture in this game, Ground furniture (carpet), Floor furniture (desk, chair, bed, cabinets, etc.), Wall furniture (painting, photo, mirror, clock, etc.) and Ceiling furniture (lamp). Each type has different rules. For example, wall furniture such as clocks or paintings can't be placed on the floor, a carpet or desk can't be placed on the wall, or a chair can't be hang off the ceiling.
  • Pathfinding is a function that use in the game map. Players can touch a point in the map, and the character will find a path to get to that point.

YRINTH



Yrinth is a virtual reality game about exploring mazes. Your goal is to find the artifact in the mazes and get out of the mazes as fast as you can. But there are monsters in there, going around the maze, guarding the artifact. Don't let those monsters catch you.

The faster you get out of a maze, the higher your score. Each maze (level) has a different difficulty and different theme. You can climb the towers in a maze to get the view of the maze from above, and to secure yourself from monsters.


Yrinth was made for Compfest 9 (2017), an indie game competition by Universitas Indonesia.
