On the Way to Earth



2065, Earth is under attack by hundreds of falling asteroids. Humanity has created a weapon to fight the "nature's will", but we realized something more dangerous is coming. An unknown army from outer space is on its way to take over our home. Save the Earth from its impending doom! Battle the enemies, and search for a new home!









During the COVID-19 lockdown, fueled by lots of spare time and boredom, I decided to make a game just for fun. On the Way to Earth: it's a simple arcade-style adventure game about swiping away the meteors from Earth. Made it all myself, the sprites and arts, SFXs and BGMs, the particle effects, and of course the codes. It has 39 Episodes (levels), 8 Worlds, 12 Bosses, and an Endless Mode for each world. Also, upload it to the Play Store just for the sake of it.


Check out On the Way to Earth on Google Play Store or the OST Here.

Impetus



Impetus is a Visual Novel game about mental health awareness among youth. It uses video games as a media for a research project of the University of Manchester, the University of Liverpool, and the Indonesia Health Ministry to understand the mental health literacy of teens 11 to 15 y.o. in Jakarta, Bogor, and Magelang.

Reality Chain



In 2021, Arsanesia Game Studio opened a separate division called Reality Chain. It focused on developing this 2D isometrical metaverse engine called 2DVerse with the capability to run, explore, and interact with other players via text or voice chat, connect your crypto wallet and edit your personal space within the world, apply your NFT as paintings, furniture, wall-textures, floor-texture, clothing, and many others.

I was in charge of implementing the tile-base map of the world, database connection, multiplayer features, crypto wallet connection, and more. Please visit https://www.realitychain.io/ to learn more or visit https://myriad.town/ to try the 2DVerse yourself.

In 2023 Reality Chain also launched https://2d.miami/, using the same engine of 2DVerse while adding the gamification aspect such as shooting monsters to gain rewards and unlocking story bits with certain currency. 2DMiami also connects to Jukiverse NFT, a popular comic character from Indonesia in which the NTFs can be worn as "avatar skin" in the game.
